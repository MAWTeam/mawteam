THE HUSTLER

The MAW team is comprised of three individuals who have worked together since 2013: Matthew Sotoudeh, Adhiv Dhar, and William Shan. In the 8th grade we came together to build two projects: Planr and Classfinder. Planr was a task management application that rewarded users with points each time they completed a task. Our vision was that users could use these points to interact with a choose-your-own-adventure story and write their own. Unfortunately, our plans fell through due to a lack of available narratives. A little bit more of a success story, Classfinder, an application that allows students in-between grades to view and share their classes for the upcoming school year, has gone through 5 different redesigns and has cumulatively attracted over 1000 users.
